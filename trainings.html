Trainings and Classes

Learn the art of gemstone craftsmanship with our expert trainers. Discover the secrets behind the creation of stunning jewelry pieces.

Short Lapidary Training

Unleash your creativity by mastering the art of lapidary work. Our expert trainers will guide you through the intricate process of shaping, polishing, and transforming raw gemstones into breathtaking masterpieces. You will learn cutting-edge techniques for precision cutting and polishing while gaining hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and equipment.

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Fees: MK600,000
  • Date: @ your convenience

Lapidary Classes

Take your lapidary skills to the next level with our comprehensive classes. This advanced program covers complex cutting techniques, advanced polishing methods, and artistic design principles. You'll work on a variety of gemstones and create more sophisticated pieces while developing your unique style.

  • Duration: 10 days
  • Fees: MK1,200,000
  • Date: @ your convenience

Gemology (Basic)

Build a strong foundation in the fascinating field of gemology, perfect for enthusiasts, hobbyists, or professionals looking to deepen their knowledge of gemstones. In this course, you will gain insight into gemstone classification and identification, learning about their properties, origins, and grading systems.

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Fees: MK600,000
  • Date: @ your convenience

Jewellery Classes

Turn your passion for gemstones into stunning jewelry creations with our specialized jewelry-making classes. You will learn the basics of jewelry design and metalworking while mastering techniques for setting gemstones securely and beautifully. The program offers opportunities to experiment with various materials.

  • Duration: 10 days
  • Fees: MK1,200,000
  • Date: @ your convenience

Mining Safari (visits)

Dive into the thrilling world of gemstone mining with our guided mining safaris and experience the journey of gemstones from the earth to the market. Visit active mines across Malawi and witness the excavation processes up close.

Services Included:

  • Access to various mines across Malawi (gold mines and different gemstone mines)
  • Transportation to different mining sites
  • Accommodation arrangements near mining sites (including camping facilities where necessary)
  • Guidance from experienced miners at the mining sites
  • Export facilitation for gemstones found or purchased during the safari

Important Notes:

  • Travel involves rough roads and basic facilities in some locations
  • Mining safaris are available year-round

What You Need (At Your Cost):

  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
  • Sunscreen and sunhat
  • Food

Export Taxes:

  • Rough gemstones: 10% of declared value
  • Cut and polished gems: 5% of declared value
Mining Safaris are carried out throughout the year. Contact us for scheduling and pricing information.
